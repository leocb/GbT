Welcome and thank you for downloading the de_train spawn-menu!

This addon creates the section "Counter-Strike: de_train" in your spawn-menu.
It features 74 useful props such as tanks or barrels with nuclear/biohazard signs, trains, wagons other useful things.

Installation
============

Extract the "de_train_menu" folder into your garrsmod/addons folder.
You will find the spawn-menu under the "Counter-Strike: de_train" section.

Requirements
============

Counter-Strike Source

Version
============

1.0
